About Us

Experience & Youth

David is a qualified Painter and Decorator originally from Forbes, now living in Canberra and licensed to work in NSW. He has painted since the 1990s in Sydney, the Blue Mountains, Canberra and Jervis Bay and has a background in jazz, blues, and rock ‘n roll as a drummer. As a painter, David comes highly recommended for his work across residential and small commercial properties.

Recently son Luka has come into the business with a unique perspective that has enriched our approach. Luka is studying for his Bachelor of Interior Architecture (Honours), adding a dynamic layer to our projects. Together they specialise in residential projects – building connections and transforming ideas, drawing from our extensive experience, innovative spirit and strong family bond.

McDade Painting is highly regarded for its clear communications with precise quotes and invoices, quality and timely completion of work.
