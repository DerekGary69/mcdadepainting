The

Waterfront Cottage

Jervis Bay, New South Wales

Positioned on the edge of the water, this charming cottage needed a fresh coat of paint to restore its beauty and protect it from the elements. A carefully selected color palette was used, taking tones from the surrounding landscape to create a harmonious and inviting space. The timber-boarded walls were painted and sealed to ensure longevity and durability, while the windows were scraped and enamelled. The result is a stunning transformation that complements the natural beauty of the waterfront setting.
