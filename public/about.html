About Us

Experience & Youth

We're David and Luka McDade, a father and son painting and decorating team. With an impressive 40 years of combined experience, we blend traditional craftsmanship with a contemporary touch.

I'm David, originally from Forbes. I've been painting since the 90s and also boast a background in jazz, blues, and rock & roll as a drummer. It's been a joy to bring Luka into the business and his unique perspective has enriched our approach. Notably, Luka is completing his BA in interior architecture, adding a dynamic layer to our projects. Together we specialise in residential projects - building connections and transforming ideas, drawing from our extensive experience, innovative spirit and strong family bond.
