About Us

Experience & Youth

We're David and Luka McDade, a father and son team deeply passionate about the art of painting and decorating. With an impressive 30 years of combined experience, we seamlessly blend traditional craftsmanship with a contemporary touch. I'm David, originally from Forbes, and I've been painting since the 90s, also boasting a background as a jazz drummer. It's been a joy to bring Luka into the business six years ago, and his unique perspective has truly enriched our approach. Notably, Luka is completing his BA in interior architecture, adding a dynamic layer to our projects. Together, we don't just create spaces; we build connections and transform ideas into tangible beauty, drawing from our extensive experience, innovative spirit, and a strong family bond.
