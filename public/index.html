Art Deco Bondi Apartment

Step into our recently completed interior project – an Art-Deco Bondi apartment completely refreshed. Ceilings, now refreshed with a pristine coat, exude an air of spaciousness and light. The bathroom, a sanctuary of relaxation, features carefully chosen tones that enhance its serene ambiance. The kitchen, a hub of creativity and nourishment, boasts a vibrant color scheme that invigorates the space. But the transformation doesn't stop there – doors, picture rails, and skirting have been lovingly re-stained to harmonize with the overall aesthetic. With every brushstroke and every thoughtful detail, this project breathes new life into the apartment, encapsulating the sophistication and allure of Art-Deco design.
