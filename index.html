Featured Project

The

Waterfront Cottage

Jervis Bay, New South Wales

Positioned on the edge of the water, this charming cottage needed a fresh coat of paint to restore its beauty and protect it from the elements. A carefully selected color palette was used, taking tones from the surrounding landscape to create a harmonious and inviting space. The timber-boarded walls were painted and sealed to ensure longevity and durability, while the windows were scraped and enamelled. The result is a stunning transformation that complements the natural beauty of the waterfront setting.

About Us

Experience & Youth

David is a qualified Painter and Decorator originally from Forbes, now living in Canberra and licensed to work in NSW. He has painted since the 1990s in Sydney, the Blue Mountains, Canberra and Jervis Bay and has a background in jazz, blues, and rock ‘n roll as a drummer. As a painter, David comes highly recommended for his work across residential and small commercial properties.

Recently son Luka has come into the business with a unique perspective that has enriched our approach. Luka is studying for his Bachelor of Interior Architecture (Honours), adding a dynamic layer to our projects. Together they specialise in residential projects – building connections and transforming ideas, drawing from our extensive experience, innovative spirit and strong family bond.

McDade Painting is highly regarded for its clear communications with precise quotes and invoices, quality and timely completion of work.

Our Services

Interiors

Our interior painting services cover a wide range of areas, including walls, trim, ceilings, and woodwork. We repair and fill holes, cracks, and imperfections before applying a fresh coat of paint to transform your space into a warm and inviting environment. Choosing the right colors for your interiors can be a daunting task. That's why we offer color consultation services to help you select the perfect shades that complement your style and enhance the overall ambiance of your space.

Exteriors

We are experienced in painting various exterior surfaces, including walls, eaves, gutters, fascias, patios, windows, and decks. We pay attention to detail and prepare the surfaces properly, filling gaps, repairing rot, and removing old paint before applying a fresh coat to protect your home from the elements. We work closely with our clients to understand their preferences and provide personalized solutions that meet their needs.

Woodwork

Our woodwork services include painting, staining, and refinishing various wooden surfaces, such as doors, windows, trims, and cabinets. We remove old paint and finishes, repair any damage, and offer yearly or bi-yearly maintenance to keep your woodwork looking fresh and new.

Hardware Refurbishment & Replacement

Our hardware refurbishment and replacement services include restoring door handles, window locks, and other fixtures to revitalise your home's original charm. We often remove and polish brass hardware, as well as removing "landlord special" painted handles and hardware; restoring them to their former glory.
