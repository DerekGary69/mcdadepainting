Recommended Products

We recommend a variety of professional-grade products to ensure a high-quality finish. Our preferred brands include Haymes Paint and Porter's Paint. These Australian made and owned products are not only reliable but also offer low odour and quick drying time, making them ideal for your next painting project.

Haymes Paint is a family-owned Australian business that has been around for over 80 years. Their range of paints is designed to withstand the harsh Australian climate, making them perfect for both interior and exterior use. Haymes Paint is known for its durability and quality, making it a popular choice for both professionals and DIY enthusiasts.

For the majority of standard applications, such as walls, ceilings, and trim, we use Haymes Paint. However, when it comes to colour selection, we defer to Porter's Paints.

Porter's Paints offers an extensive colour variety, allowing us to meet the unique preferences and requirements of our clients.

We use Porter's Paints specifically for specialty applications such as feature walls, cabinetry, and furniture. Their range of colours and finishes allows us to create unique and custom looks for our clients. However when it comes to standard applications, such as walls, ceilings, and trim, we use Haymes Paint.

Undercoat

McDade Painting recommends and uses Haymes Ultracover for its superior coverage and adhesion. It's perfect for preparing surfaces for painting and ensuring a long-lasting finish.

Ceiling

Made by Haymes, this ceiling paint is designed to provide a flat finish that hides imperfections and reflects light. It's perfect for brightening up any room.

Deck & Timber Stain

Haymes Dexpress is a high-quality decking oil that penetrates deep into the timber, providing long-lasting protection against the elements. It's perfect for revitalizing and protecting your deck.

Kitchen & Bathroom

Haymes Kitchen & Bathroom paint is specially formulated to resist mould and mildew, making it perfect for high-moisture areas. Its low odour and easy clean-up make it ideal for any project.

Stain Blocker

Zinsser Undercoat Oil-Based Stain Blocker is perfect for covering stubborn stains and sealing odours. It's ideal for use on water stains, smoke damage, and more.

Gap Sealant

Polyfilla Gaps & Cracks is a flexible gap sealant that's perfect for filling gaps and cracks around the home. It's easy to apply and dries quickly, making it our top choice.

Brushes

McDade Painting recommends using quality brushes for a professional finish. We prefer Monarch brushes for their durability and precision, ensuring a smooth and even coat every time.

Masking Tape

Haymes Painter's Masking Tape is perfect for achieving clean lines and protecting surfaces during painting. Its low tack adhesive ensures easy removal without damaging your paintwork.
